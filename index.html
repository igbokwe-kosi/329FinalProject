About Us

Hi, welcome to our web page. You’re probably wondering who and what we are. Well, we are a cybersecurity company founded by several incredibly poor university students. We took a cybersecurity class in the fall of 2023, and one of our lectures was about password security. We were told “Ac0rn!” was a bad password (which is why we DON’T use it), however, “incredibly safe acorn themed password” is an incredibly strong password (which is why we use it). That shook us more than an earthquake. Who would guess that?

With that, we realized how BAD our entire society is at cybersecurity. We realized that nobody really knows anybody about how we should be doing things. We don’t know about what is dangerous, why is it dangerous, who makes it dangerous, and how we can make it safer. That is our goal. If you look out into a forest it is covered and trees, and for those to grow they need to be protected when they are most vulnerable; nature gave them the acorn. That’s us. A small thing, but hopefully we can help trees grow everywhere.

Please, use the site and use it to protect yourself, your future and your family. Thank you for your patronage – Acorn Security Services team
