Top 10 Ways to Stay Safe

By Seth Pullishy

Pick an attacker and a defender

The attacker will guess while the defender laughs at how bad they are at guessing

The defender picks an amount of words

I would suggest 3 or 4, allows you to paint a picture of what you’re imagining, but DO NOT SHOW THE ATTACKER

Let them guess. Tell them when they are wrong. Smile as they lose hope.

If you want a rematch and want to make them suffer don’t tell them how long it is. Use all 100. Mix it up.

Math explanation:

If we choose 1 word, they have a 1% chance. If we choose 2 words they have a 0.0001% chance of getting it on the first guess. If we choose all 100 in a random order, there are 100! possibilities, or in other words there are 93,326,215,443,944,152,681,699,238,856,266,700,490,715,968,264,381,621,468,592,963,895,217,599,993,229,915,608,941,463,976,156,518,286,253,697,920,827,223,758,251,185,210,916,864,000,000,000,000, 000,000,000,000 possibilities, think about how low the chance of that is. This doesn’t even include if you can use the same word an unknown amount of times!
